HOW TO PLAY

A white elephant is a holiday gift exchange game in which participants play to take home the best gift.



How to Participate:

  1. Find a gift!

    2. a. Pick out a gift that most audiences will enjoy, or choose a gag gift to make the game more interesting.

  2. Submit your gift!

    3. Fill out our RSVP form to enter your gift into the game! Be sure to have a link to your gift or pictures ready.

  3. Read the rules!

    4. For all of our offcial rules, check out the section below before Christmas Day.

  4. PLAY!

    5. Log in to our Zoom on Christmas Day (12/25/22) at 5:00pm EST to participate!



Rules and Reminders

  1. Upon submitting your gift, you will receive a confirmation email from our host. Please remember your gift number!
  2. Before the game begins, your name will be placed into a wheel that will randomly draw each participant’s name to determine the order that gifts will be opened.
  3. During the game, you will see a grid of every gift hidden under a numbered gift box.

    4. To ensure that you do not choose your own gift, avoid choosing the gift number sent in your email.

  4. When the game begins, each participant will open gifts in the order they were chosen by asking the host to open a box of their choosing from the grid on the screen.
  5. Once the gift is revealed, the participant will be asked if they would like to keep their gift or “steal” it from another player.

    6. The person that goes first will be able to “steal” at the end of the game!

  6. Each participant gets one chance to open a gift and one chance to steal.
